Studio 808

The mission of STUDIO 808 DANCEPROJECT llc is to teach and train individuals of all ages the art of dance. The studio will educate, encourage and nurture these students who find dance appealing, fun and a learning experience. STUDIO 808 DANCEPROJECT will support them in fulfilling their development through classes, programs and performances.

Playground HI

Through a wide range of resources, The Playground equips dancers with the knowledge of dance through interactive and foundational teachings. We train dancers to master their styles of dance and have them explore other genres so that they learn teamwork and integrity through the artistic styles of our choreographers. The Playground: A community for dance and a dance for the community. We empower the dance community by sharing the knowledge of dance through an inspirational, artistic, and diverse environment.

HyperSquad

Our philosophy is to take the kids off the streets and into street dancing. Street Hip Hop is what we specialize in to include Jazz, B-Boy and B-Girl, Poppin, Krumping, and more. Our youngest dancers start at 3 yrs old. Our out-reach program includes Free class for all high school students on Saturdays from 2 - 3pm. Also FREE is every Friday night's 8-9:30pm B-Boy and B-Girl Open session. We are accepting donations so we can enroll more kids who have that passion to dance. We have an annual concert and we enter major international dance competitions as well as perform for parties and private events.
