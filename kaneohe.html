Kaneohe

Kāneʻohe is a census-designated place included in the City and County of Honolulu and located in Hawaiʻi state District of Koʻolaupoko on the island of Oʻahu. In the Hawaiian language, kāne ʻohe means "bamboo man".

24/7 Dancestudio

24-7 Danceforce, a non-profit organization, exists to continually motivate and create as a company in the art of dance and life; to always grow as individuals, becoming people of good character and integrity, always compassionate of others; to inspire others to strive for their highest potential.
